Rutgers University–New Brunswick: Location, Contact, and Mission

Location: Rutgers University–New Brunswick is located in the heart of central New Jersey. Its main address is 7 College Ave, New Brunswick, NJ 08901, USA. The campus is known for its historic roots and vibrant academic community.

Mission and Values:
Rutgers University–New Brunswick is dedicated to providing a world-class education while fostering an inclusive environment for all students. Its missions include:

  • Advancing knowledge through innovative research and scholarly activities.
  • Offering high-quality education that prepares students for leadership roles in a global society.
  • Promoting public service and engagement with local and global communities.
  • Championing diversity, equity, and inclusion in all aspects of campus life.

Academics at Rutgers University–New Brunswick

Top Majors and Programs:
Rutgers University–New Brunswick offers a diverse range of programs, with top majors including:

  • Business
  • Computer Science
  • Engineering
  • Biological Sciences
  • Psychology
  • Pharmacy
  • Political Science

Curriculum Overview:
Students are required to complete general education classes, which include courses in writing, math, social sciences, and natural sciences. In addition to these, each major has its own set of core courses.

Faculty Expertise:
Rutgers–New Brunswick faculty are leaders in their fields, including renowned experts in healthcare policy, molecular biology, and artificial intelligence. Faculty members are actively engaged in cutting-edge research and mentorship.
